Play

Chameleon

Online!

Reveal the Chameleon's identity without disclosing the secret word. If you happen to be the Chameleon, your goal is to blend in with the other players, evade detection, and deduce the secret word.

Every round, each player announces a one-word clue about the secret word. At the end of the round, players vote out who they think is the most suspicious. The Chameleon then reveals if they were successful.

Too many or few players?
3 players: If the Chameleon gets caught, they get two guesses at the secret word.
7 or 8 players: After everyone says their word, the host can change the board so that the Chameleon has a harder time figuring out the secret word.

Disclaimer: This is a non-profit project made by Max Zhang and some friends out of pure love of the original game. It is meant to provide ease of play/access while maintaining the overall idea. The project is not affiliated with any of the official publishers of The Chameleon in any way. Check out the official website here. This project is also heavily inspired by another version of online Chameleon. It aims to fix inconsistent disconnections present in the other version at the time of this project's creation, and is also more aimed towards in-person play.
